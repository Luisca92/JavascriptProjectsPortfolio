Hangman
Play the game!
Hangman is a word-guessing game where the player tries to guess the word selected by the computer. The player starts by selecting a letter, and if it's in the word, the computer reveals its position. If the letter is not in the word, part of a hangman is drawn. The game continues until the word is guessed or the hangman is completed.
